Projects

Runaway - Game

Runaway is a 3D narrative game built in Unreal Engine 5 over the course of 2025 with a team of ~11 Massey University students.

I lead the design and implementation of key gameplay systems (camera, event management, etc), coordinated version control via Github, and aided in Project Management.
I also managed all Audio and Music requirements – created and implemented all music and audio assets in the game via FMOD.

Incorporated - Game

Incorporated is a short top-down shooter made over 4 weeks by myself and 2 other students as part of the COSC360 Game Development paper at the University of Otago in early 2024.

My contribution involved designing and implementing key gameplay systems such as player movement, weapon inventory, and weapon firing, designing and implementing all levels, and also the creation and implementation of all sound effects including music.
